<div class="project-section-card">
<h4>Data & Modeling</h4>
<p>Raw experimental data was messy and inconsistent, requiring extensive cleaning before any model training could happen. I enriched the dataset using LLM APIs to generate synthetic data from scientific literature, cross-validating against experimental results to ensure quality. From there, I benchmarked multiple embedding strategies for material representation, then pre-trained on a large general-purpose dataset and fine-tuned for the specific screening task. Hyperparameter tuning was done via Bayesian optimization, with training infrastructure running on AWS and Kubernetes.</p>
</div>

<div class="project-section-card">
<h4>Results & Deployment</h4>
<p>The final model achieved a <strong>50% improvement over baseline LLM models</strong>, directly reducing the number of experiments needed in the discovery pipeline. Models were converted into MCP tools with ~1 minute inference time, making them accessible to experimentalists without ML expertise and enabling an active learning cycle where predictions continuously guide experimental decisions.</p>
</div>

<div class="project-section-card">
<h4>Collaboration & Impact</h4>

Led the project end-to-end, gaining hands-on experience in stakeholder alignment, project planning, and translating research into production-ready tools.</p>
