Projects

A collection of projects spanning machine learning, computational science, and creative systems.

ml & ai

Accelerating Material Discovery with Machine Learning @ Lila Sciences

End-to-end ML pipeline for large-scale material screening — from messy experimental data to deployed MCP tools — achieving a 50% improvement over baseline LLM models.

Reaction-Aware Agent Prediction via Local Neural Models

Reaction class-specific D-MPNN and FFN models trained on 15M patent reactions for chemical agent prediction, achieving ~20% improvement over global baselines.

Predicting Antibiotic-Compound Synergy Against MDR Bacteria Using Machine Learning

LightGBM + ChemBERTa classifier trained on 1.3M drug combinations to predict antibiotic synergy against multidrug-resistant bacteria, achieving 80% accuracy and AUROC of 0.88.

Topological Feature-Driven Machine Learning for Mechanically Stable MOF Discovery

ML framework predicting MOF bulk modulus from topology, chemistry, and geometry features — screening ~435k candidates with 75% MD-validated accuracy.

SolvaNET: Predicting Solvation Free Energies with Machine Learning

Gradient boosting model combining Morgan fingerprints and RDKit descriptors to predict logK for solute-solvent pairs, achieving R² = 0.973 on cross-validation.

Optimizing Solar Panel Waste Recycling Facility Placement in Massachusetts

Geospatial optimization model using p-median and coverage formulations to site solar panel recycling facilities across Massachusetts for minimal transport cost and equitable access.

science

CRISPR-Cas9 LNP Delivery for FOP Gene Therapy

Muscle-targeted lipid nanoparticle system for CRISPR-Cas9 RNP delivery to correct the ACVR1 R206H mutation causing Fibrodysplasia Ossificans Progressiva.

Copper-Doped Natural Zeolites for Low-Cost Methane Mitigation (Patent Acquired)

Cu-exchanged natural clinoptilolite catalysts achieving complete methane conversion at 500°C at a fraction of the cost of synthetic zeolite benchmarks.

Synthesis and Characterization of MOF/Carbon Nitride Composites for Photochemical CO₂ Reduction to Fuels

Fabrication of MOF-carbon nitride nanosheet composites as visible-light photocatalysts for sustainable CO₂-to-fuel conversion.

Resistivity-Based PFAS Detection via Fluorous Polyaniline Lateral Flow Sensor

F-PANI sensor on nitrocellulose membranes for field-deployable PFAS detection, with transport modeling reducing assay time to ~13.6 minutes.

Sensor-Based Assessment of CO and Particulate Matter Exposure in Indoor Tunnel Environments

Portable sensor deployment to characterize occupational CO and PM exposure levels in underground transit tunnel environments.

Formulation-Dependent Structural and Swelling Characterization of Polyacrylamide Hydrogels

Systematic study of how monomer and crosslinker concentration governs polyacrylamide hydrogel network structure and equilibrium swelling.

MOF Instability and Polymer Infiltration in Amine-Functionalized UiO-66/PIM-COOH Mixed Matrix Membranes

Investigation of framework degradation and polymer chain infiltration in UiO-66 mixed matrix membranes for gas separation.

fun

Immigrate: A Choice-Based Interactive Narrative System

A choice-based interactive narrative game that simulates the immigration experience through branching, consequence-driven storytelling.

TravelAI: An Intelligent Personal Travel Planning Agent

Conversational AI agent that builds personalized, multi-day travel itineraries by combining preference modeling with real-time data.
